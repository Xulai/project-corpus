Document control - Google Drive

All group members had access to a folder named 'CO600' on Google Drive in which all documents related to the project were shared. This included minutes, designs, requirements and pseudo code.

We chose Google Drive due to its ease of access - content could be created easily within the folder using Google Docs and having granted read/write permissions to all group members, information was readily available to everyone.
