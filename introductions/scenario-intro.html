Introduction to our scenarios

After gaining an insight on some key factors about our project through the Con-Ops, we were able to create scenarios of where and how our device might be set up and used.

Our scenarios made us think about how individuals could interact with our sensor and device, which in turn helped us in deciding further requirements of the system as a whole in the design stages. For example, the fact that we would need to deploy our sensor at a location containing a bridge or some sort of support near the river.
