Device and area stats

A big part of our API was to quickly access meaningful data coming from our sensors. In order to ensure this, our API provided a range of endpoints to query the following information on devices and areas:

  • The average of all readings.
  • The last reading sent to the database.
  • The last previous reading difference (as a value and a percentage) and average of the previous reading difference percentages.
  • A count of how many readings.
  • An average of all previous reading differences.
  • The average of the power & the last power sent to the database.

There was a specific endpoint for all statistics: /stat, and for statistics of a specific device or area: /device/ID/stat, /area/ID/stat
