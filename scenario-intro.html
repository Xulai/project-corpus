Introduction to our scenarios

Our scenarios came about after creating our Con-Ops (a first basic step outlining the requirements of our system).

They really got us into thinking about how our sensor and device would be used, which in turn helped us in deciding further requirements of the system as a whole in the design stages. For example the fact that we would need to deploy our sensor at a location containing a bridge or some sort of support near the river.

In Scenario 1, we imagine that our sensor is deployed somewhere near a river, and we explain how exactly the deployment is done for the first time. We also explain the initial testing procedure given the first deployment.

In Scenario 2, we imagine the behaviour needed to confirm that the device is working and sending readings on its own.
