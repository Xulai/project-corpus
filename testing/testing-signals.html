Testing for signals

This page will display our testing of 4G signals and The Things Network gateway coverages around Canterbury river areas.

Testing for 4G signals around Canterbury river

Method:

The group met up to walk around different public areas alongside the river and test out data signals within each spot where we saw a potential mounting spot for the sensor. We took screenshots of our Google Map location so we could later reference it, which also captured the data signal in that particular spot. We also decided to take photos of each area we stopped at to later on combine all the data to provide this visualiation.

We started off near Pound Lane, following the river up to just before The Causeway street and then following it back down to Toddler's Cover Playground - totalling up to a 0.5 mile route.
